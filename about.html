THE MODERN ATELIER --

Reconnecting to his past while envisioning an idyllic romantic era, Kobi Halperin introduces a women's collection to the market rich in luxurious textiles with an emphasis on crafted artisan details.

Born in Israel in the 1970's and raised in a home with deep Eastern European roots, Kobi grew up surrounded by hand carved ornamented furniture, embellished tapestries and beautifully framed paintings detailed with brushed metals. Kobi continues to draw inspiration from the intricate details of his nostalgic past while giving today's woman a modern and feminine look.
